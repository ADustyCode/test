# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## Account & Login

**Q: I forgot my password. How can I reset it?**
A: Go to the Login page and click on "Forgot your password?". Enter your email address, and we will send you a password reset link.

**Q: Why didn't I receive the verification email?**
A: Please check your Spam or Junk folder. If you still can't find it, you can request a new verification link from the verification page after logging in.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**
A: Yes. Log in to your account, go to **Settings**, and look for the "Update Email" section.

## Job Applications (Jobseekers)

**Q: How do I know if my application was viewed?**
A: Employers may update the status of your application. You can check the status (e.g., "Pending", "Accepted", "Rejected") in the **My Applications** section.

**Q: Can I apply for the same job twice?**
A: No, you can only submit one application per job posting.

## Job Posting (Employers)

**Q: Is there a fee to post jobs?**
A: (Customize based on business logic) Currently, posting jobs is free for all registered employers.

**Q: How do I stop receiving applications for a job?**
A: You can either delete the job or edit it. We recommend deleting or closing the job if you have filled the position.

## Technical Support

**Q: The page is not loading correctly.**
A: Please try clearing your browser cache or try accessing the site in Incognito/Private mode. If the issue persists, contact support.
