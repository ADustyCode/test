# User Manual (End User Documentation)

## 1. Getting Started

### 1.1 Registration
1.  Navigate to the Homepage.
2.  Click on the **Register** button.
3.  Choose your role:
    -   **Jobseeker**: If you are looking for a job.
    -   **Employer**: If you want to post jobs and hire.
4.  Fill in the required information (Name, Email, Password).
5.  Click **Register**. You may need to verify your email address via the link sent to your inbox.

### 1.2 Login
1.  Click on the **Login** button.
2.  Enter your registered email and password.
3.  Click **Login** to access your dashboard.

---

## 2. Jobseeker Guide

### 2.1 Searching for Jobs
1.  Go to the **Job Board** menu.
2.  Browse the list of available jobs.
3.  Use the search bar or filters to find jobs by category or keyword.

### 2.2 Applying for a Job
1.  Click on a job title to view details.
2.  Click the **Apply** button.
3.  (Optional) You may be asked to upload a resume or cover letter.
4.  Confirm your application.

### 2.3 Managing Applications
1.  Navigate to **My Applications**.
2.  Here you can see the status of all your submitted applications (e.g., Pending, Accepted, Rejected).

### 2.4 Managing Profile
1.  Go to **Profile**.
2.  Click **Edit Profile** to update your personal information, skills, and resume.
3.  Go to **Settings** to change your password or email address.

---

## 3. Employer Guide

### 3.1 Posting a Job
1.  Navigate to the **Jobs** dashboard.
2.  Click **Create Job**.
3.  Fill in the job details:
    -   **Title**: The name of the position.
    -   **Description**: Detailed responsibilities and requirements.
    -   **Category**: Select the industry.
    -   **Salary**: (Optional) Salary range.
4.  Click **Submit** to publish the job.

### 3.2 Managing Jobs
1.  In the **Jobs** dashboard, you see a list of your posted jobs.
2.  Click **Edit** to update job details.
3.  Click **Delete** to remove a job posting.

### 3.3 Managing Applications
1.  When a jobseeker applies, you will receive a notification.
2.  Go to the specific Job's **Applications** view.
3.  Review the applicant's profile and resume.
4.  Update the application status (e.g., **Accept** for interview, **Reject**).

### 3.4 Employer Profile
1.  Go to **Profile** to update your company information.
2.  This information is visible to jobseekers when they view your job postings.
